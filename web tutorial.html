IMPORTANCE OF HAND WASHING
Hand hygiene is the most important and effective method of preventing the spread of transmissible diseases. Teaching appropriate hand washing to children can promote wellness and have numerous benefits in a wide variety of settings such as learning institutions including schools. Keeping hands clean through is one of the most important way to prevent sickness and spreading germs to others. Hand washing can fight the spread of common cold, meningitis, bronchitis, influenza, hepatitis A, and most types of infection diarrhea. Conventional hand washing with soap is a component of hand hygiene program to reduce the risk of infection through hand contact.
